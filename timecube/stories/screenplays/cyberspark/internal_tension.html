Internal Tension

Internal Tension is the first of 3 screenplays in the CyberSpark trilogy. It will be released a few scenes at the time on Tapas and Kindle Vella

Synopsis - Mika and Lkaka just want to live a normal life, without the terrors of war. After an explosion at the Skel building leaves Lkaka severely injured, Mika sets out to find who set the bomb. Unbeknownst to her, the country is on a path towards civil war. There is more going on here than she realizes. War is on the horizon.
